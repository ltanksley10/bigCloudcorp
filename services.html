High-Availability & Recovery:

High-availability? Fault-tolerant? RPO or RTO? More buzzwords and acronyms, but they also have very important meanings. Obviously, you want your datasets and workloads secured from loss or corruption, but how to do it? Backups? Well, what kind of backups? Cloud native Disaster Recovery (DR) plans keep your vital systems going no matter what. Fault-Tolerant means your application or website is built to stay online in the event of a disaster, such as infrastructure failure at physical data centers. High-Availability means your application is built to scale to the fluctuating trends that makes the world turn.

Architecture:
