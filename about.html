So...what is BigCloudCorp?

BigCloudCorp is just that, a corp of dedicated people helping others bring big ideas to the cloud. Our focus is set on enabling people in the age of cloud. BigCloudCorp could be thought of as a cloud technology consulting team with MSP services in the future, but for now this will only be a place to come for information. We’re excited to bring a community of intelligent developers, engineers, architects, or tinkerers together soon. Our goal is to provide information enabling rapid product deployment and scale to the market’s demand with an automated approach.
