So...what is BigCloudCorp?

BigCloudCorp is just that, a corp of dedicated people helping others bring big ideas to the cloud. Our focus is set on empowering people in the age of cloud. BigCloudCorp could be thought of as a cloud technology consulting team with MSP services in the future, but for now this will only be a place to come for information. We’re excited to bring a community of intelligent developers, engineers, architects, or tinkerers together soon. Our goal is to provide information enabling rapid product deployment and scale to the market’s demand with an automated approach or help the solo dev working on the side project. We want to share the info we have, and that’s what we’re trying to do. Who knows, one day BigCloudCorp could launch like a rocket! We could add a chat section and become the next social media tyrant. Tyrant? I think we meant social media giant :) Maybe you’ll be able to login to a help-desk portal when you load up BCC next time. Electric cars, anyone? Or we could stay a simple info retreat for your child’s science fair project. One thing is for sure: we really like the cloud and telling others about it. If you want to know more about the cloud and think you’re ready for cumulo-technology, then contact the Corp.
